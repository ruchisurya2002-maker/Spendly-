Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 14, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains what information Spendly collects, how it is used, and the choices you have regarding your data when you use our personal expense tracking application.

1. Data We Collect

We collect the information you provide directly, such as your name, email address, and login credentials when you register. We also collect the expense records, categories, and other financial entries you add while using Spendly.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used to provide and improve the core features of Spendly, such as displaying your expenses, generating summaries, and maintaining your account. We do not use your data for advertising and do not sell it to third parties.

3. Data Storage

Your account and expense data are stored securely and retained for as long as your account remains active. We take reasonable technical measures to protect your data, though no method of electronic storage can be guaranteed as completely secure.

4. Third Party Services

Spendly may rely on third-party services, such as hosting or infrastructure providers, to operate the application. These providers only process data as necessary to deliver their service and are not permitted to use your data for their own purposes.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please contact us through the support channels listed in the app.
